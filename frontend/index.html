Expected Value Analysis
📖 How EV Works

Expected Value (EV) = (Model Probability × Decimal Odds) − 1
A positive EV means the bookmaker is offering better odds than our model suggests — a "value bet".

No-Vig probability removes the bookmaker's margin from their implied odds so the comparison is fair.

Kelly Criterion gives the mathematically optimal fraction of your bankroll to stake. We apply half-Kelly (50%) for safety and cap at 25% of bankroll.

📐 How ELO Works

The ELO formula: E(home) = 1 / (1 + 10^((Ra−Rh)/400))

After each match, ratings update by K×(actual − expected). K=20 means each result can shift a rating by at most 20 points. The system naturally rewards beating stronger opponents and punishes losing to weaker ones.

These ELO values feed directly into the XGBoost model as EloHome and EloAway features.
